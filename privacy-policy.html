Privacy Policy

Introduction

We prioritize protecting the privacy and personal data of our customers and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information related to our online store of PC and console games. By using our website and services, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Data

When using our services for purchasing PC and console games, we may collect personal data such as your first and last name, contact details, and other information you provide voluntarily. These data are collected when you fill out forms, register for services, or contact us via email or other communication channels.

2. Usage Data

We may collect information about how you interact with our website. This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and date and time of access. This information helps us better understand user needs and improve our content.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze site usage. You can manage cookie settings in your browser, but disabling them may affect website functionality.

How We Use Your Information

1. Service Delivery

Your personal data is used to provide our services related to selling PC and console games, including processing orders, responding to inquiries, and personalizing content to meet your preferences.

2. Communication

We may use your contact information to inform you about updates, promotions, and other information related to our game store. You have the right to opt out of such communications at any time.

3. Analytics and Research

Usage data and cookies are used to analyze trends, manage the website, and improve content. This helps us provide materials better suited to the needs of gamers.

4. Legal Compliance

Your data may be used to fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If required by law, data may be disclosed to relevant authorities.

Data Sharing

1. Third-Party Services

We may share your personal data with external service providers who assist us in operating the website or delivering our game sales services. These providers are obligated to protect your data and cannot use it for other purposes.

2. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets, your personal data may be transferred to the new entity. You will be notified, and your rights will remain intact.

3. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your personal data if required by law or in response to valid legal requests by authorities.

Data Security

We protect your personal data through appropriate technical and organizational measures. Despite our efforts, we cannot guarantee complete security, so please use strong passwords and be cautious when sharing your data.

Your Rights

1. Access and Correction

You have the right to access your personal data and request corrections if your data is inaccurate or incomplete. Please contact us for updates.

2. Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal data from our systems, provided there are no legal requirements to retain it.

3. Processing Restrictions

You have the right to request restrictions on processing your data under certain circumstances, such as disputes about accuracy.

4. Data Portability

You can request a copy of your personal data in a format that allows you to transfer it to another service provider.

5. Objection

You may object to the processing of your personal data for marketing or other legitimate interests. We will respect your choice and adjust processing accordingly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We recommend reviewing this page regularly for the latest information.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we process your data, please contact us. We are ready to assist you.
